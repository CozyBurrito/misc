The surrounding Xs are not needed but they make
it so that you do not have to check both for a 
block and the end of the board when figuring
out the extent of a word.

Since the largest number in the puzzle is 29, we
can easily make do with a group of size 29.

The following is a convenient table for figuring out what clue symbols
correspond to what clue numbers:
0123456789abcdefghijkmnopqrst
          1111111111222222222
01234567890123456789012345678
